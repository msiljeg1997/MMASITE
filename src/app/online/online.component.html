MIXED MARTIAL ARTS

Mixed Martial Arts or MMA is the world’s fastest growing sport, experiencing rising popularity with the notoriety and world wide expansion of the Ultimate Fighting Championship, UFC.

It is a full contact combat sport that combines striking and grappling, both standing and on the ground.

MMA makes use of techniques from various martial arts including Wrestling, Muay Thai and BJJ. We encourage our students to build a strong foundation in striking, wrestling and grappling before learning to combine these disciplines in an MMA context, to form a complete and well rounded skill set.
